Introduction

Pratyay is a student research conference for budding research enthusiasts to contribute to and know the research community. The event provides the student researchers an opportunity to showcase their talent and come up buzzing with ideas in varied fields.


The event encourages the students to think and ponder over the latest developments in the field of computer science and contribute their part in the journey to the unexplored domains.


Conference Themes

Papers are invited from students of B.Tech / B.E. / M.Tech / M.E. / IDD / IMD / B.Sc / M.Sc / BCA / MCA courses in Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

Submission

  • Papers must be sent to review.adhyayan@itbhu.ac.in.

Instructions to Authors

  1. Paper should be in strictly IEEE Format and must be in word document (.doc) format only.
  2. Full length paper should not exceed 6 pages.

Judging Criteria

  1. The authenticity of the idea.
  2. The relevance of the idea with the respect to the present work in that domain.
  3. The presentation given by the author.
  4. Viva by the judging panel.

Registration

  1. Registration Fee : INR 600
  2. At least one author should register to include the paper in any of the technical sessions in the conference.
  3. If more than one author wants to attend the conference, they must register individually.
  4. Accomodation shall be provided as per availability in IT BHU Hostels.

Important Dates

  • Full Paper Submission Mar 18, Sun
  • Notification of Acceptance Mar 19, Mon
  • Camera-ready Paper Submission Mar 21, Wed
  • Conference Dates Mar 23-25, 2012

B.Tech / B.E. / B.Sc. / BCA and IDD / IMD upto 3rd year

M.Tech / M.E. / M.Sc. / MCA and IDD / IMD from 4/5th year
