Welcome to Adhyayan 2012

Adhyayan is the Annual Student Conference and Online Coding Festival of Department of Computer Engineering, IT BHU.


Adhyayan is a unique concept featuring both search for academic research excellence as well as tough programming challenges, quizzes and demonstrations from the field of Computer Science and Engineering.. Adhyayan shall be held from March 12-25, 2012.


Apart from the Pratyay student conference which shall be held from March 23-25, we shall have the following events as a part of the coding festival :

  • Prayaas : an online algorithm intensive and mathematical programming challenge
  • Parikshan : a technical quizing event (both online & onsite)
  • Pradarshan : an onsite software demonstration contest

Organized by

Computer Engineering Society

Department of Computer Engineering
