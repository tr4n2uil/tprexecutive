Introduction

Parikshan is an online and onsite technology quizzing event covering different areas of the broad spectrum of Computer Science. This event will test the knowledge of the participants in varied domains in the field of computer science .


This will be a non-programming event and facilitates greater exposure to the questions from all the core fields of computer science.


Details

Scope

The questions will be selected from varied areas in the field of computer science. Some of them may be mentioned as follows:

  1. Web Technologies
  2. Programming Languages.
  3. Database Systems
  4. Computer Networks
  5. Cyber Security
  6. Operating Systems
  7. and many more ...

Participation

  • You must be registered on our website to participate.
  • It is an individual event.
  • Only students shall be eligible for prizes.

Rules

  1. There shall be one online trial round, two online main rounds and one onsite final round.
  2. Each online main round shall have 60 questions to be answered in 30 minutes.
  3. The online trial round shall have 40 questions to be answered in 20 minutes.
  4. It would be similar to rapid-fire quizes. The more questions you answer correctly, the more points you earn.
  5. For each corect answer, 2 points shall be awarded and for wrong answer, there will be -1 negative marking.
  6. You shall be allowed to submit only once.
  7. The time interval for submission in each round shall be strict and hence participants are required to complete their submission before timeout.
  8. Solutions shall be available just after the rounds. You shall also be able to submit feedbacks for the round.
  9. Scoreboard shall be available after two days.
  10. The scores of both online rounds shall be combined to yield your total score which will be the basis for qualification into onsite final round.
  11. Details of onsite round shall be updated soon.
