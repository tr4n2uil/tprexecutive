Mathematical Challenge

This challenge shall put to test your knowledge of mathematics combined with computational programming abilities that will together reach you to solutions of the problems.


Details

Participation

  • You must be registered on our website to participate.
  • This contest is open for all the students and professionals.

Rules

  1. The challenge shall be similar to Project Euler
  2. The contest consists of about 8 to 12 mathematical puzzles to be solved in 5 hours.
  3. At the end of 90 and 180 minutes of the contest,a surprise problem will be disclosed. The submissions for the surprise problems will be accepted only within 30 minutes of the release of problems.
  4. There will be two cases for each of the problems - a large case and a small case and there will be separate answers for each of them. The submission for the large case will be allowed only after that of small case has been accepted successfully but both the cases will be disclosed at the time of problem release.
  5. Answers for both the cases will be evaluated as soon as they are submitted but only the result for small case will be intimated immediately while the result for the large case will be intimated after the contest gets over.
  6. Answers for each of the problems will be positive integers. You need to submit only the answers for each of the problems.
  7. A maximum of 20 submissions for any problem are allowed. But only the last submission will be used for evaluation.
  8. Event organizers may ask for the explanation of any solution submitted by you, failure to which may lead to disqualification.
