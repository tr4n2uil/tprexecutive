Procedure

  1. The "Training and Placement Office" sends invitaiton to Companies/Organizations along with the required information
  2. Interested Company/Organization has to download, fill the Job Recruitment Form and send it to the Training and Placement office
  3. If the Company/Organization wishes to visit the campus and conduct a Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) before the interview sessions, they can send a request along with the preferred dates
  4. The JRF is made available to the students, along with all the other information furnished by Company/Organization. Companies can ask for the resumes of interested students and shortlist students
  5. Dates are alloted to the company for their reqruitment process i.e. interview/gd etc.
  6. The company/organization is required to furnish the final list of students preferably on the date of interview or the next day.Core Engineering industries
